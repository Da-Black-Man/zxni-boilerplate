Do you have what it takes ?

  • Must have valid state Driver's License Class A
  • Recent verifiable driving experience for the last 12 months
  • Max 3 points on your MVR within the last 3 years
  • Must be at least 23 years old
  • Must be able to drive either Automatic or Manual Transmissions
03.

Driver Work Perks .

Excellent Benefits

We offer a variety of benefits that will keep you healthy and happy including 100% company paid medical, dental and vision insurance. Also, $20K company paid Life Insurance policy and a 401k profit sharing and savings plan with a company match.

Solid Work-Life Balance

We offer 8 paid holidays, paid vacation time, and work is local with flexible schedules - so you will have peace of mind knowing that you can be home every night! If you want to work line haul, you will be home every other night.

Stable Work

We will keep you busy. We also offer overtime after 8 hours/40 hours for qualified employees. With our vast array of customers, we will match you up with a customer that fits best.

Career Development

We aim to promote learning and personalized growth. We do this by providing ongoing training so you can have the tools and resources that foster success. When you are assigned to one of our customer accounts, you will typically remain with that customer. There are other opportunities that will arise to further develop your driving career though.

Safety-Oriented Team

Our culture is centered around family and safety. We operate safely and we take care of our drivers so you can take care of your family. OSHA authorized, former driver supports all driver training and safety needs.

24/7 Support

We provide 24/7 coverage from our dispatchers. You’ll always have the support you need.
